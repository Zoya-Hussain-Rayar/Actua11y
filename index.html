Who We are:

Actually is a pioneering platform dedicated to bridging the gap between organizations and disabled individuals.

Our mission is to make the digital world more accessible and inclusive by leveraging the unique insights and experiences of disabled people.

Our primary services is to provide a Digital Accessibility Assessment. By partnering with us, you are not only enhancing your digital assets but also promoting inclusivity and innovation.

At Actually, we believe that accessibility is a fundamental right, and we are here to help you achieve it.
